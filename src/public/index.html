What are TED Talks?

TED Talks are online video presentations featuring passionate speakers from many walks of life. The presentations originally focused on topics related to Technology, Entertainment, and Design, but now they're also about culture, politics, and everyday things.

For many of us, TED Talks have been widespread and culturally relevant for as long as we can remember, but the TED organization itself actually comes from much humbler beginnings.

The first TED conference, organized by Richard Saul Wurman, took place in 1984.
